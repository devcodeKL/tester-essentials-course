Pikachu

Bio

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon known for its cute appearance and electrical powers. It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.

Characteristics

  • Electric sacs in its cheeks store electricity for its attacks
  • Raised tail indicates Pikachu's mood and health
  • Capable of releasing powerful electric shocks
  • Weak against Ground-type Pokémon

Evolution

Pikachu evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship. It then evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
